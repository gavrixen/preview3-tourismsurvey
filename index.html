About us

Welcome to our specialized platform designed exclusively for the next generation of tourism and hospitality professionals. We believe that excellent education relies on a strong connection between classroom theory and real-world application. This tool gathers and analyzes the diverse experiences of tourism students navigating their degrees, field activities, and industry placements. By capturing these insights, we help shape a more responsive, impactful educational journey that better prepares students for the global workforce.

Our Mission

To empower tourism students by providing a dedicated digital platform to voice their academic and practical experiences. We aim to collect high-quality, actionable feedback that enables educators and industry partners to continuously improve training programs, optimize curricula, and better equip students for the evolving demands of the global tourism and hospitality landscape.

Our Vision

To become the definitive platform for student insight in tourism education, fostering a collaborative ecosystem where data-driven academic learning and exceptional practical experience align perfectly to produce world-class tourism professionals.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this survey platform?
This platform is designed to bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world hospitality and tourism training. By collecting insights on your academic courses and hands-on practical experiences, we can help educators optimize the curriculum to better prepare students for the global tourism industry.
How long does it take to complete the survey?
We know student schedules are packed, so the survey is streamlined. It typically takes about 5 to 10 minutes to complete, depending on how much detailed feedback you want to share about your practical experiences.
Is my personal data safe, and will my answers be confidential?
Absolutely. Data privacy is a top priority for us. Your specific personal information is completely protected and will never be shared. All survey results are aggregated for academic analysis, meaning your responses are analyzed as a group rather than tied to you individually. For more details, you can view our full Privacy Policy in the website footer.
Do I need an account to take the survey?
Yes, creating an account helps ensure data integrity, meaning one valid student equals one set of responses. It also allows you to save your progress if you need to step away and finish the survey later, or log back in to view your submission history.
Who can I contact if I experience technical issues?
If you run into any bugs, trouble logging in, or problems submitting your responses, please use the "Contact Us" link in the footer to send a quick message to our development team.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 21, 2026

I. Introduction

Welcome to the Tourism Management Academic & Practical Experience Survey Platform. We are deeply committed to protecting the privacy, confidentiality, and security of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal, academic, and contact information. By registering an account and using this platform, you explicitly consent to the data practices described in this policy.

II. Information We Collect

To fulfill the platform’s objectives as an institutional research tool and maintain a secure environment, we gather specific data categories during your two-step registration and subsequent survey participation:

Account & Authentication Credentials: Full name (First Name and Last Name), institutional email address, and account password.

Academic Identification Details: Official Student ID Number and Year Level. This data is mandatory to verify your eligibility as a student researcher and to categorize survey analytics.

Contact & Compliance Details: Primary contact number, date of birth, gender, and complete permanent home address (including House Number, Street, Barangay, City/Municipality, Province, and ZIP Code).

Crisis & Emergency Records: Full name, primary contact number, and defined relationship of your designated emergency contact.

III. How We Use Your Information

The information collected on this platform is utilized strictly for the following purposes:

Academic and Curriculum Analysis: Survey responses are aggregated and anonymized to evaluate educational experiences, track industry placements, and optimize the tourism and hospitality curriculum.

Identity Verification & Data Integrity: Student ID numbers and institutional emails are verified to ensure a strict "one valid student, one set of responses" standard, preventing fraudulent or duplicate submissions.

Institutional Accountability & Security: Contact information, permanent addresses, and compliance details are securely logged to protect the digital infrastructure. In accordance with platform terms, these records provide traceability to protect against unauthorized access, malicious data tampering, or intentional disruption of the system.

Emergency Responsiveness: Designated emergency contact profiles are maintained exclusively to ensure student safety during official institutional tracking or system-related crises.

IV. Data Sharing and Confidentiality

Your privacy is a fundamental priority. We enforce strict data separation boundaries:

Anonymized Reporting: All research insights, survey results, and feedback data are compiled and presented to educators or industry partners strictly as aggregated statistics. Your individual answers will never be personally tied back to your identity or student profile in any public or academic report.

No Commercial Sharing: We do not sell, rent, trade, or distribute your personal information to third-party commercial entities. Information is only disclosed to institutional authorities if required by official disciplinary procedures stemming from system tampering or as mandated by legal compliance.

V. Data Storage and Security

We implement robust administrative and technical security measures to safeguard your records against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is stored in secure, encrypted databases. While we take maximum precautions to protect your digital footprint, users are reminded that they share responsibility for system security by maintaining the confidentiality of their account passwords.

VI. Account Management and Retention

Your student profile and compliance records will be retained for the duration required to complete the ongoing academic research cycle and satisfy institutional accountability guidelines. Users may manage their profiles through the student dashboard. If you wish to request the deletion of your account or inquire about your stored records, please reach out via the technical team contact framework provided in the platform footer.

VII. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy as the platform evolves or institutional regulations change. Any modifications will be posted directly on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage users to periodically review this policy to stay informed on how we protect student data.
